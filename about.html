About 'PARK'

Wichinpong Sinchaisri (วิชินพงศ์ สินชัยศรี)

I'm currently a third-year graduate student at MIT pursuing a Sc.M. in Computation for Design and Optimization (CDO), under the supervision of Professor Georgia Perakis and Professor Yanchong Karen Zheng. My current interests lie at the interface of machine learning, behavioral decision theory, and revenue management.

Besides optimizing every part of my life, I enjoy exploring food, playing board games, traveling, taking photos (of places and food), doing creative artwork, and singing. Originally from Bangkok, Thailand, I have visited 14 countries: Singapore, Japan, Hong Kong, China, Malaysia, United States (including Hawaii, Alaska, and Puerto Rico), Bahamas, Mexico, Honduras, Belize, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, and Canada (+ transits in South Korea and Germany).
